# Contributing

Contributions are always appreciated and welcome!

At this early stage of the project, I am particularly interested in data regarding the efficacy of `flutterdocs_mcp` or any other MCP server that wraps Flutter/Dart documentation:
- Results from A/B testing with and without `flutterdocs_mcp`.
- Results from A/B testing `flutterdocs_mcp` versus alternatives.
- Examples of AI agents failing to get needed information from `flutterdocs_mcp`.
- Examples of AI agents requiring an unreasonable number of steps to get needed information from `flutterdocs_mcp`.

I am also interested in data regarding platforms and hosts that `flutterdocs_mcp` works with.

The best way to contribute feedback is by [filing an issue](https://github.com/smoyerx/flutterdocs_mcp/issues) or by [starting a discussion](https://github.com/smoyerx/flutterdocs_mcp/discussions) thread. This keeps all information in the repo where it is readily available to everyone.

I have not yet enabled pull requests on the repo, but plan to do so after completing some additional experimentation.
